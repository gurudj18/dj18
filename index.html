What is ‘Dhruva Jnanam’?

It’s an online group started for children ages five and above with the inspiration and blessings of Brahmasri Dr. Samavedam Shanmukha Sarma garu for the enablement of children to grasp and follow dharmic values and put them into practice with thorough comprehension. This aids children in amalgamating the morals, customs, traditions, and culture of Sanatana Dharma into their daily lifes with courage, confidence and clarity. This also channelizes children’s talents in various areas such as writing, oratory, singing etc., and mentoring them to achieve ‘Success with Character’ in all fields and transform into future responsible leaders.

Objectives of ‘Dhruva Jnanam’

  1. To expose children to Sanatana Dharma and its eternal values.
  2. To empower children with vision, discipline and dynamism.
  3. To inculcate the practice of Sanatana Dharma corroborating with rational and scientific thinking.
  4. To instill pride in our ancient heritage and inspire them to hand over our traditions, customs and observances to future generations .
  5. To emphasize that learning about God should be cultivated from childhood itself, exemplifying Bala Ganapati, Bala Siva, Bala Bhima, Bala Krishna, Bala Tripura Sundari, Bala Hanuman etc.
The eternal truth, profound knowledge, universality of application and magnanimity embedded in Sanatana Dharma can best be understood, when one reminisces about how young Naren filled with a temperament of rhetoric, rationality and reasoning, later got transformed into Swami Vivekananda and became a world leader.
